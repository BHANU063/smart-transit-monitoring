# Abstract

Urban public transport systems, specifically in metropolitan areas like Bengaluru, suffer from unpredictable bus timings, overcrowding, and lack of real-time passenger information. Existing solutions focus only on individual features like tracking or ETA estimation and are often costly and poorly integrated. As a result, passengers lack decision-making support, and authorities lack unified data for system analysis.

Therefore, there is a critical need for a cost-effective, integrated system that combines tracking, intelligent ETA prediction, and crowd estimation. SmartTransit fills this gap by simulating realistic public transit operations, tracking real-time traffic conditions, and providing smart boarding recommendations to improve overall transport efficiency and passenger convenience.
